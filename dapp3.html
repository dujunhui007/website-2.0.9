Introduction:

Help China is a mobile application of light-social, news and platform based on ASCH chain with the function that users can allocate and accept assignments online, chat and browse news. Help China announces demands of individuals and collective to other individuals or the collective through online matching rule so that other individuals or the collective can understand users’s demands and fulfill them online or offline. By doing this, it will increase the interaction between people and solve the existing problem of human nature in reality to increase the friendly atmosphere of mutual help for the purpose of building a friendly and kind social environment.
