Introduction:

BlockPets is a game running on the asch chain. The main design is to apply the incentive mechanism by tokens to the game.In addition, by the smart contract of asch chain, users can accumulate their virtual assets and also can prove their virtual assets. This is a completely free game which is different from others, users can enjoy any features in the game without any pay. Users can completely control their digital assets by their own strategy and time spending in the game, they can transfer, donate their digital assets freely, even more they can transfer their digital assets to another game.
